Procedure

  1. The experiment is designed only for 4 processes and 3 resources.
  2. On Simulation page, Enter the details of already allocated resources to process P1
  3. Enter the details of maximum resources required by process P1(allocated + need).
  4. Click on Submit button.
  5. Repeat step 2 to step 4 for remaining 3 processes.
  6. Enter the details of currently available resources which can be allocated to process.(Available)
  7. Click submit button
  8. Just check area below Next button to see the output of steps and observe the updations in table.
  9. Press the Next button to execute the next step. Keep pressing Next button for execution of every step.
  10. In the beginning, initial need will be calculated.
  11. Then the processes are checked repeatedly if they can be completed with the present available.
    (meaning in order: Process 1 -> Process 2 -> Process 3 -> Process 4 -> Process 5 -> Process 1 ... and so on until all processes are executed or need of remaining processes is not satisfiable with available resources).
